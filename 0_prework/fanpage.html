Bio

I'm not a fitness fantic but moreso believe it's important to exercise ona daily basis. Crossfit provides a very good framework for daily exercise.

Below I list my five basic rules or choices for fitness.

My Fitness Rules/Choices

  1. Show up / do the work
  2. Listen to instincts (xfit = body; e.g. heart, e.g. what drives ur instinct)
  3. Technique over intensity (ie volume, load, speed)
  4. Nutrition - eat real food, min sugar and processed foods, eat raw fruit/veggies, proper amount of protein
  5. Be kind, tender, supportive, fun
